# Billing Portal — FAQ

## How do I download an invoice?
Sign in to the Billing Portal, open **Billing → Invoices**, and click the download icon next to any invoice. Invoices are available as PDF and include tax details.

## How do I update my payment method?
Go to **Billing → Payment Methods → Add card**. Set a card as default with the "Make default" toggle. The next charge uses the default card.

## How do I change my plan?
Open **Subscription → Change plan**. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

## What is your refund policy?
Refunds are available within 14 days of a charge for annual plans. Monthly plans are non-refundable but you can cancel anytime to stop future charges. Submit a request from **Billing → Help → Request refund**.

## Why was my card declined?
Common reasons: expired card, insufficient funds, or bank fraud protection. Update the card under **Payment Methods** and retry. If it keeps failing, contact your bank to authorize the charge.

## How do I get a tax receipt / VAT invoice?
Add your tax/VAT ID under **Billing → Business details** before the invoice is generated. Existing invoices can be reissued once with the correct tax ID from the invoice's "..." menu.
