# Mobile App — FAQ

## I can't log in to the app.
First confirm your email and password work on the website. In the app, tap **Forgot password** to reset. If you use single sign-on, choose the same provider (Google/Apple) you registered with. Make sure the app is updated to the latest version.

## My data isn't syncing.
Pull down on the home screen to force a sync. Check that you are online and that **Settings → Sync** is enabled. Background sync requires the app to have permission to refresh in the background (iOS: Settings → General → Background App Refresh).

## I'm not getting notifications.
Enable notifications in the device settings for the app, then in **App Settings → Notifications** choose the categories you want. On Android, disable battery optimization for the app so it can wake for push messages.

## The app keeps crashing.
Update to the latest version from the App Store / Play Store. If it still crashes, restart the device, then reinstall the app. Your data is stored in the cloud and will restore after you log back in.

## Does the app work offline?
Yes. Recently viewed content is cached for offline reading. Changes you make offline are queued and uploaded automatically the next time you have a connection.

## How do I update the app?
iOS: open the App Store, tap your profile, and update. Android: open Play Store → Manage apps → Update. Enable automatic updates to stay current.
